## What the new prose needs to do

1. Acknowledge that the corporate posture toward DEI has fluctuated (post-2020 rapid expansion, post-2024 pullback), WITHOUT being timestamped — no specific years, no firm names, no specific surveys or reports.
2. Argue that the paper's central question remains central rather than peripheral.
3. Anchor that argument in things that DON'T fluctuate with corporate rhetoric: anti-discrimination law, the visibility of selection decisions to multiple stakeholders, broader employee/stakeholder expectations around representation, and individuals' own egalitarian commitments.
4. Close with the scope claim already promised in the response letter: descriptive feedback should be most influential where past selection patterns raise concerns about prejudice and less influential where those concerns are absent, muted, or explicitly rejected.
