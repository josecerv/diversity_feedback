If you want belt-and-suspenders coverage, use Option A in the Introduction and a one-sentence echo in the General Discussion: "As we note in the Introduction, our framework does not assume that initial selections are biased relative to any particular benchmark; we predict feedback to shift subsequent behavior only on dimensions that carry an implicit injunctive norm."

## Recommendation

Option A (Introduction) is the cleanest. The assumption is theoretical — it belongs at the point where the theory is being set up. Putting it in the GD risks reading as a defensive concession after the fact rather than a principled scope statement.
